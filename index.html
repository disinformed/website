Hello world

Hi. We are a collaborative group of researchers, artists, writers, engineers, and cultural producers interested in finding breaches for creation in the sea of mass information. We engage with new possibilities for artistic creation enabled by emerging technologies, as well as with critical assessment of the personal, social and political implications such modes of production introduce. Materials for our projects include images, videos, AI algorithms, digital art, social media, NLP and blockchain as well as online publications, websites and social media interventions. We promote creative research and development that challenges traditional forms of representation and distribution. Humanistic and ethical issues related to software are becoming more and more prominent. We are in hope that the discussion, experimentation and elaboration we produce can provide a better understanding of the context as well as a theoretical backdrop for the ongoing development of innovative technologies, making these more humanistic, and socially just.
